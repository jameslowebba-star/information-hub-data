Meet the Founder

Sifiso

Mining Professional & Media Entrepreneur

Sifiso works in South Africa's mining industry by day and builds Information Hub by night. Based in Gauteng, South Africa, he launched this platform out of a simple frustration: news moves fast, but most of it comes wrapped in noise, bias, or clickbait.

As a crypto enthusiast who follows precious metals and tracks global markets, I needed a news source that was fast, verified, and straight to the point. When I couldn't find one that met that standard — I built it.

Why Information Hub Matters to Me

“I come from an industry where precision matters — in mining, bad data can cost lives. I brought that same standard to news. Every story on Information Hub is fact-checked against multiple sources before it goes live. No speculation, no clickbait, no noise.

I built this for people like me — professionals, investors, and everyday people who want to know what’s happening in the world without having to dig through junk to find it. Whether it’s a breaking geopolitical event, a gold price surge, or a crypto market shift, you should be able to get the facts in seconds.

Information Hub is where speed meets accuracy. That’s the promise.”

— Sifiso, Founder
