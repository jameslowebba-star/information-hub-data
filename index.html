About

Our mission and editorial values

Our Mission

Information Hub exists to give independent thinkers — investors, analysts, entrepreneurs, and informed citizens — access to real-time intelligence that used to be reserved for institutional players. We believe that the quality of your information determines the quality of your decisions.

What We Cover

Global News

Verified world news — geopolitics, conflict, diplomacy, and political developments that shape markets.

Market Intelligence

Real-time market data, analysis, and macro context to help you understand what drives asset prices.

Africa Focus

Business, politics, and investment intelligence across Africa — from the JSE to mining, fintech, and political risk.

Crypto & DeFi

Bitcoin, Ethereum, regulation, DeFi, and the evolving intersection of crypto with traditional finance.

Our Values

Accuracy First

We only publish what we can verify. Speed matters, but not at the cost of accuracy. Every story is sourced.

No Agenda

We report facts and context, not narratives. Our editorial line is independent of political or commercial interests.

Market Relevance

We prioritize stories with real market, policy, or economic implications. We filter out noise so you don’t have to.

Meet the Founder

Sifiso Mining Professional & Media Entrepreneur

By day, Sifiso works deep in South Africa’s mining industry. By night, he builds Information Hub. Based in Gauteng, he created this platform because he was tired of watching critical global events get buried under clickbait, spin, and noise.

A crypto enthusiast who tracks precious metals, commodities, and global macro shifts — I needed an intelligence source that moved at the speed of markets but held the accuracy of a research desk. It didn’t exist. So I built it.

Why Information Hub Matters to Me

“In mining, bad data costs lives. I brought that same zero-tolerance standard to news. Every story on this platform is verified against multiple sources before it goes live. No speculation. No clickbait. No noise.

I built this for professionals, investors, and everyday people who refuse to be the last to know. Whether it’s a geopolitical flashpoint, a commodity price shock, or a crypto market shift — you deserve the facts in seconds, not hours.

We’re not chasing money — we’re chasing impact. Money is a by-product of that impact. Information Hub is where speed meets accuracy. That’s the promise, and that’s the standard.”

— Sifiso, Founder
